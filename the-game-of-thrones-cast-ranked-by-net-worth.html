10 Isaac Hempstead-Wright (Bran Stark)

Isaac Hempstead-Wright's Net Worth (Estimated): $4 Million (£3 Million)

Isaac Hempstead-Wright appeared as Bran Stark on Game Of Thrones, his first appearance as a young child when the show first started airing.

One of the youngest actors, Hempstead-Wright started off earning a smaller amount than many of the show's older stars. But portraying Bran Stark over a long period enabled the young actor to earn money at a slower pace.

Though he wasn't the highest paid member of the Game Of Thrones cast, Hempstead-Wright's net worth was estimated in the range of $4 million or £3 million as of May 2019, per British tabloid The Daily Express.

Hempstead-Wright's net worth evidences his potential in the entertainment industry.

9 John Bradley (Samwell Tarly)

John Bradley's Net Worth (Estimated): $5 Million

John Bradley assumed the role of Samwell Tarly, appearing on Game Of Thrones for its eight season run.

Initially, Bradley started off on Game Of Thrones with lower pay, occupying a more minor role. But as Samwell became more critical to the story, Bradley's compensation grew accordingly.

By the end of Game Of Thrones, Bradley reportedly earned about $500,000 per episode.

A British actor, John Bradley's net worth is estimated at around $5 million as of early 2024.

8 Gwendoline Christie (Brienne of Tarth)

Gwendoline Christie's Net Worth (Estimated): Between $4 Million And $7 Million

Gwendoline Christie portrayed a noble knight named Brienne Of Tarth on Game Of Thrones.

Christie turned in a remarkable supporting performance in the star-studded drama. When Christie joined the team as a new member, she initially got paid less than actors who had been on the show for a longer stretch.

But her increased screen time and the relevance of her character's role provided her with leveralge to negotiate higher compensation, and by the end of the season, she was receiving $500,000 for each episode.

As of early 2024, Gwendoline Christie's net worth is estimated to stand at between $4 million and $7 million, due in large part to her star turn on Game Of Thrones.

7 Maisie Williams (Arya Stark)

Maisie Williams' Net Worth (Estimated): Between $6 Million And $10 Million

Maisie Williams demonstrated robust acting skills as a strong Arya Stark on Game Of Thrones, in a breakout performance that was amazing considering her young age.

Williams, a child actor on the show, initially earned a modest sum of around $175,000 for each episode in which she appeared, like Sophie Turner.

However, GoT fans adored Arya, and as such, Williams' pay significantly increased as the seasons kept coming.

As of early 2024, estimates of 26-year-old Maisie Williams' net worth vary tremendously; typically, it is approximated at between $6 million and $10 million.

6 Sophie Turner (Sansa Stark)

Sophie Turner's Net Worth (Estimated): Between $10 Million And $12 Million

Sophie Turner brought the innately good Sansa Stark to life for Game Of Thrones, becoming a massive fan favorite during the show's run.

As one of the youngest main cast members, Turner initially received a lower rate of about $175,000 per episode. Like Christie, Turner's ongoing lead role enabled her to eventually negotiate higher per-episode compensation.

Turner earned almost $12 million from this long-running role by the time the show concluded in 2019; at the time, she had just turned 23. Turner's wealth demonstrates how Game Of Thrones provided its youngest actors with a significant opportunity to bolster their Hollywood earning power.

As of 2024, Sophie Turner's net worth is estimated at between $10 million and $12 million.

5 Kit Harington (Jon Snow)

Kit Harington's Net Worth (Estimated): $14 Million

On Game Of Thrones, Kit Harington played Jon Snow, a good-hearted character who was popular with fans. He soon became one of the main heroes on the show.

Jon Snow is a significant character on Game Of Thrones, because he is a secret heir. Like Emilia Clarke, Harington began with a smaller per episode salary, but later made about $500,000 per episode in the final seasons.

In December, ScreenRant reported that Kit Harington was set to return to HBO in 2024, but not for a Game Of Thrones related project; Harington was slated to appear on the network's Industry.

As of early 2024, Kit Harington's net worth was estimated at $14 million.

4 Nikolaj Coster-Waldau (Jaime Lannister)

Nikolaj Coster-Waldau's Net Worth (Estimated): $16 Million

Nikolaj Coster-Waldau portrayed Jaime Lannister on Game Of Thrones, and he turned in a performance that warranted an Emmy nomination.

Coster-Waldau was significant to the show's success as a fighter and later, a fearsome "Kingslayer." While Coster-Waldau wasn't making the most money initially among his cast mates, his rate went up significantly in the last seasons, to about $500,000 for each episode.

In late 2023, CBR.com reported that Coster-Waldau was set to appear in a new BBC project, King And Conqueror, on which he would also serve as an executive producer.

Nikolaj Coster-Waldau's net worth is estimated consistently at around $16 million.

3 Lena Headey (Cersei Lannister)

Lena Headey's Net Worth (Estimated): $16 Million

Lena Headey's portrayal of the evil queen Cersei Lannister on Game Of Thrones made her character one that fans loved to hate throughout most of the show's run.

Headey got praise from critics for her tricky and cruel role as Cersei. She became one of the most important characters on the show – and made $1.2 million for each episode during its last season.

As of early 2024, Lena Headey's net worth is widely estimated at around $16 million.

2 Emilia Clarke (Daenerys Targaryen)

Emilia Clarke's Net Worth (Estimated): $20 Million

Emilia Clarke portrayed the iconic Daenerys Targaryen, or Mother of Dragons, on HBO's Game Of Thrones.

Clarke, a main character, soon rated as a fan favorite, and her role was essential to the plot. At first, she didn't make much money, but Clarke earned about $500,000 for each episode in the show's final seasons.

On December 30th, Deadline Hollywood reported that Clarke (along with her mother) had been awarded a prestigious MBE (Member of the Order of the British Empire) for their work in founding a charity to support people with brain injuries.

Emilia Clarke's net worth is estimated at around $20 million in 2024.

1 Peter Dinklage (Tyrion Lannister)

Peter Dinklage's Net Worth (Estimated): Between $25 Million And $30 Million

During the show's eight seasons, Peter Dinklage was known as the much-loved character Tyrion Lannister on Game Of Thrones.

Dinklage's outstanding acting won him several Emmys in the "Outstanding Supporting Actor In A Drama Series" category. Dinklage's Tyrion Lannister, a key, beloved character, became one of the most well-compensated cast members.

By the time the last seasons of the show rolled around, he was making a reported $2 million per episode, reflecting his value to the network and on the show itself.

Peter Dinklage's net worth is estimated at between $25 million and $30 million as of early 2024.
