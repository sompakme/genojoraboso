Nolan North is an American actor known for his works as a voice actor on projects like Uncharted, Assassin's Creed, Batman: Arkham Origins, Batman: Arkham City, Lego Marvel Super Heroes, Marvel Heroes, Ultimate Marvel vs. Capcom 3, Spider-Man: Shattered Dimensions, Deadpool, and Marvel vs. Capcom 3: Fate of Two Worlds. As an actor, North appeared in the sequel of the 2009 film Star Trek, 2013's Star Trek Into Darkness. 

Nolan Ramsey North was born on October 31, 1970 in New Haven, Connecticut, U.S.. Since 1999, he has been married to Jill Murray with whom he has two children. He studied journalism at the University of North Carolina, which offered him a baseball scholarship.

North is also noted for his voice works in numerous video games which include Prince of Persia, Assassin's Creed, Lord of the Rings: War in the North, Ar tonelico II: Melody of Metafalica, Call of Duty: Black Ops, Call of Duty: World at War, Transformers: War for Cybertron, and Marvel: Ultimate Alliance.
