Update On Brock Lesnar In Madden 2005

Publish date: 2024-08-19


First and foremost, thanks to all of you who emailed me on this subject.

Brock Lesnar is in the Madden 2005 game. He is in the updated roster that you get online. We’re told he plays the DT position. Lesnar wears the number 69 and he’s around 67 or 68 overall. He has a lot of potential if you start him. You’ll find him in free agency.

We’re told that he is ONLY available in the official Madden NFL 2005 Roster updates on the November 21st and December 18th editions. He is a Free Agent DT and his overall rating is a 69.

You also must have a PS2 online adapter. If you create a player with his name in a regular game, the announcers will say his name.
