Britney Spears Says &quot;My Boyfriend Cheated on Me&quot; During Vegas Concert

Publish date: 2024-07-07

She's not holding back! Britney Spears publicly addressed the widespread rumors that her now ex-boyfriend David Lucado cheated on her this past weekend, confirming the talk while on stage in concert for her Piece Of Me Las Vegas residency. 

Dressed in a sexy black one-piece outfit, Spears spoke to her fans, shouting from the stage, "Well, by now everyone knows my boyfriend cheated on me."

Spears, 32, split from her boyfriend of 17 months last week after video reportedly surfaced of Lucado kissing another woman. While she had confirmed that she was no longer with Lucado, tweeting "Ahhh the single life," Spears hadn't spoken about her ex's alleged infidelity.

Despite the dissolution of her relationship, the "Perfume" singer seems to be in good spirits. Before she mentioned during the show that she needs her girls and is on the hunt for a "really hot guy," Spears highlighted the silver lining of her situation.
