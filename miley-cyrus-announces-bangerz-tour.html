Miley Cyrus Announces Bangerz Tour

Publish date: 2024-08-10

Controversial pop princess Miley Cyrus will be coming to a city near you. The “We Can’t Stop” singer recently announced the dates of her upcoming Bangerz tour, named after her latest album.

The road trip kicks off on Valentines’ Day, Feb. 14, in Vancouver BC before hitting all the major spots across North America and wraps up on April 24 in Uniondale, NY. Expect a night of twerking, explosive visuals, crazy costumes and Miley on her worst behavior. Tickets go on sale Nov. 16 at livenation.com.
