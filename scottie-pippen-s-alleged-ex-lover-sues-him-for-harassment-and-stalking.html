Scottie Pippens Alleged Ex-Lover Sues Him For Harassment And Stalking

Publish date: 2024-09-01

A woman by the name of Chevette Valentine is suing Scottie Pippen for alleged harassment and stalking. According to Page Six, legal documents were filed on Wednesday in Illinois by Valentine, who claims to be an ex-fling of the NBA legend. 

In the suit, not only is Valentine suing for the above, but has also alleged that Pippen and his brother, Carl, raped her, which resulted in the birth of her late son, Devonte Pippen, who died in 2011.

Once her alleged romance with Pippen came to an end, the woman said that the former athlete “used his celebrity as a Chicago Bulls player” to “stalk, harass and torment” her. She also claimed the situation caused her “severe mental and emotional issues,” the loss of several employment opportunities, and even the loss of housing.

Valentine claimed that the 58-year-old used connections with local, state and federal employees to cancel and falsify police reports, in order to make her seem “mentally ill.” She is now seeking $250,000,000 million from Scottie, the Bulls, and his family members.

Back in 2019, she also sued Pippen for $9,999, which was “the maximum allowed amount in small claims court” at the time over travel expenses to see him while he was married. She admitted that she didn’t know he was married to Karen McCollum at the time, and would visit Scottie on the road due to him promising to pay her back.

Additionally, she claimed that Pippen’s most recent ex-wife, Larsa, “caused her suffering” at the time.

Pippen separated from McCollum in 1990 and wed Larsa in 1997, before divorcing her in 2018.
