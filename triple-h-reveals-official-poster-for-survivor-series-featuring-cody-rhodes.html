Triple H Reveals Official Poster for Survivor Series Featuring Cody Rhodes

Publish date: 2024-08-29

After achieving immense success with the Crown Jewel Premium Live Event, WWE is gearing up for its next biggest PLE of the year. The Premium Live Event in question is the Thanksgiving Tradition for the Stamford-based promotion, Survivor Series War Games. Recently the Chief Content Officer Triple H shared the official poster for the major event.

As mentioned above, this past weekend was the WWE Crown Jewel Premium Live Event. The event saw Solo Sikoa potentially retiring John Cena. The social media Megastar Logan Paul also captured his first major championship in the Stamford-based promotion by defeating Rey Mysterio. Rhea Ripley, Seth Rollins, and The Tribal Chief Roman Reigns all managed to successfully retain their respective championships.

Now, with Crown Jewel in WWE’s rear-view mirror, the promotion is getting set for the next Premium Live Event, Survivor Series War Games. It is one of the major PLEs of the promotion. The event will be taking place in Chicago at the All State Arena on the 25th of November. Before even a single match was announced, the show has been completely sold out.

With less than three weeks left for the show, the Chief Content Officer of WWE, Triple H took to Twitter to share the official poster of the event.

“Let the games begin… SurvivorSeries: #WarGames descends upon Chicago’s @AllstateArena
on Saturday, Nov. 25, streaming live at 8e/5p @peacock @WWENetwork,” Triple H tweeted.

Last night on Raw, Zoey Stark won a Battle Royal to earn a shot at Rhea Ripley and her title at the PLE. The Miz will also be wrestling Gunther for the Intercontinental Championship. Also Adam Pearced announced that The Judgment Day, Rollins, Zayn, Cody Rhodes, and Jey Uso will be part of the Men’s War Games match.
