Kyla Pratt Brother Doheen Pratt and Family

Publish date: 2024-08-11

Kyla Pratt brother Doheen Pratt is four years younger than her. Doheen accompanied Kyla to Its A Wonderful Lifetime Holiday Party at STK Los Angeles.

Kyla started her career as an actress at just seven years old. According to Los Angeles Times, her career began after her grandmother passed her few snapshots around a plane that caught the eye of a model scout.

Pratt made her acting debut in the 1993 television series Where I Live as Brittany. Since her acting debut, the actress has amassed 77 acting credits on her IMDb profile. Kyla is best known for her appearance in One on One, Dr. Dolittle, The Proud Family, and Recovery Road.

She is now the voice of Penny Proud in Disney’s animated series The Proud Family: Louder and Prouder. She is staring alongside Aldis Hodge in the series. She also appeared alongside Marques Houston and Tim Reid in the television series Sister Sister.

Besides her acting career, Kyla is also in Disney’s singing group Disney Channel Circle of Stars. She sang the cover version of Circle of Life. Pratt is also the singer of It’s All About Me, A Dream Is a Wish Your Heart Makes, and There’s No Time Like Christmas.

Doheen Pratt Is 32 Years Old

Kyla Pratt brother Doheen Pratt is 32 years old. Doheen is four years younger than his sibling Kyla.

Doheen and Kyla attended a holiday party event of It’s A Wonderful Lifetime at STK Steakhouse in Los Angeles on October 22, 2019. She shared an image from the event the following year in June to welcome Doheen into the 30-year-olds’ club. 

Doheen celebrates his birthday on June 15 every year and was born in the year 1990. The two siblings appear to have a deep bond between them as Kyla previously shared an image with Doheen and her other sibling on her Instagram account.

While Kyla grew up a star child and made headlines for all the good reasons, Doheen was not known for particularly good causes. He had a troubled past for which he spent eight years starting at the age of 13 years old.

According to CBS News, Doheen was convicted of a drive-by shooting in California that claimed the life of 19-year-old Nicole Williamson the day after Thanksgiving in 2001. He served his sentence at a California youth authority facility before being released in 2013 when he was 22 years old.

He does not have much of a social media life and keeps away from sharing his personal information with the general public. However, he does make a rare appearance through his sister’s Instagram account. 

Their Parents Are No Strangers To Showbiz Industry

Kyla Pratt parents Kecia Pratt-McCullar and Johnny McCullar are former actress and baseball player respectively.

Kecia and Johnny welcomed Kyla as their first in Los Angeles, California, on September 16, 1986. Kecia McCullar is an actress best known for her appearance in One on One where she shared the screen with her daughter in episode 20 of season 1. 

However, according to her IMDb profile, she does not have any other experience as an actress. Apart from acting, Kecia worked full-time as a schoolteacher, reports Los Angeles Times. 

Kyla shared a rare picture of her mother holding her when she was just a toddler on the occasion of Mother’s day in 2014. She thanked her mother for making her the woman she has grown into in the Instagram post. Her mother has a private Instagram account with her username @k_pizzle5. 

Moreover, her father Johnny McCullar played baseball as a semi-professional. Details about his baseball career are not available on the internet but he changed his career to a stockbroker. 

Kyla does not seem to be very close with her father as she has not shared any images with him on her social media account. Instead she has wished her beau for being the best father for her two daughters. 

She Has Three More Siblings

Kyla Pratt siblings include her sister Kejahn Tiye and two brothers. Pratt is the eldest of five children.

Apart from Doheen Pratt, Kyla is also the elder sister of three more siblings. She has a younger sister named Kejahn Tiye and another younger sibling named Allen Pratt, according to Kecia Pratt’s Facebook profile. 

She shared an image collage of her sister Kejahn to wish her 18th birthday on September 24, 2015. Kejahn is already 25 years old as of 2023. She has a Facebook account but has not shared anything since 2016. 

Allen Pratt is a social media influencer and internet personality in his own right. He has over 13.3k followers on his Instagram account but has not shared anything with his followers yet. He is found using Facebook actively. 

According to Los Angeles Times, Kyla is the eldest of five children. We have found three of her four children so far and her remaining younger sibling does not have a trace on the web. 

Kyla Has Two Daughters

Kyla Pratt kids are the adorable daughters Lyric Kirkpatrick and Liyah Kirkpatrick. Kyla shares both of her children with her fiance.

The Insecure actress welcomed her first daughter Lyric Kirkpatrick on November 17, 2010. She welcomed her second daughter Liyah Kirkpatrick three years later on August 5, 2013. However, she took her time to let the world know about her little ones.

Lyric Kirkpatrick

The 12-year-old was born on November 17, 2010. 

She is the eldest of the two daughters Kyla shares with her long-term partner and fiance. The pair welcomed their daughter before getting engaged in 2011.

Despite being just 12 years old, Lyric already has nearly 11k followers on her Instagram account. She is found on the social media platform with her username @lyrickaikp and has shared 17 posts so far. Her Instagram bio reads, she loves (blue heart emoji) her mommy and her daddy. 

According to Amo Mama, Kyla took her time to announce the arrival of her firstborn. She did not make any public appearances during her pregnancy and revealed the arrival of the bundle of joy nearly a year later. 

Liyah Kirkpatrick

Liyah Kirkpatrick joined her sister three years later as Kyla’s second child.

The 9-year-old was born on August 5, 2013. The actress repeated her act of hiding away from the public and taking nearly a year to announce the news on the internet about her second pregnancy and child as well. 

Liyah is also available on Instagram with her username @liyahastarkp and her bio reads daddy’s girl. Unlike her sister, she is yet to find fame on the picture-sharing social media platform as she only has 42 followers. Her account is also private at the moment. 

However, she makes frequent appearances on the internet through her parent’s and elder siblings’ Instagram accounts. 

Kyla Pratt Is Engaged To Danny Kirkpatrick Since 2011

Kyla Pratt baby daddy Danny Kirkpatrick is dating her for nearly two decades now. Danny got down to his knees in 2011 to seal the deal with the actress.

The pair started dating in 2005 and welcomed their first child five years later in 2010. Danny took the opportunity to take Kyla officially off the market by putting a ring on her finger (he also got off the market with that ring). 

However, the pair have not technically and legally married yet. According to Distractify, Kyla revealed they are not in a rush to get married and are in a very good position with their relationship. 

She also revealed that marriage brings unnecessary pressure into the relationship and she wants to work on their romance before jumping into the pressure. She also revealed she does not want to get hasty and married just to end up having a divorce. 

Kyla’s fiance Danny is better known as KP and is a reality television star and the tattoo artist. He is a frontrunner in the television reality show Black Ink Crew: Compton as the first tattoo shop owner in the city. 

The show’s official synopsis on the IMDb profile reads, the show is centered around KP and IAM Compton crew. He has also appeared in other projects including Mystery ER and Morbid: A Love Story among others.
