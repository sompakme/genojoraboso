'RHOSLC' Stars Heather Gay and Whitney Rose's Ups and Downs

Publish date: 2024-09-02

When The Real Housewives of Salt Lake City premiered in 2020, Heather Gay and Whitney Rose were the best of friends, but their relationship has since gotten rocky.

Heather and Whitney are third cousins, but their connection runs deeper than a distant family relation — they bonded through the years over motherhood, entrepreneurship and their upbringings in the Church of Jesus Christ of Latter-day Saints.

During season 1 of RHOSLC, costar Lisa Barlow gave them the nickname “Bad Weather” because of their rowdy dynamic. However, their friendship started to show cracks during season 3 when Heather refused to corroborate Whitney’s claim that costar Angie Harrington told them Lisa slept with someone for courtside tickets to the Utah Jazz.

The pair seemingly made up while filming season 3 of Ultimate Girls Trip in July 2022, but the détente had ended by the time they attended BravoCon three months later. When season 4 premiered in fall 2023, Heather and Whitney had called a truce.
