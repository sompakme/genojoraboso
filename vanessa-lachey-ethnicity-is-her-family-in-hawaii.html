Vanessa Lachey was born on November 9, 1976, in Blue Ash, Ohio, to parents of English and Irish descent.

Vanessa is the youngest in her family and has an elder brother Vincent. Vincent is two years older than his sister. 

Her family had to migrate to several cities during her childhood. She lived in Nevada and then moved to California.

Later, her family moved to Florida, Germany, and Japan. Finally, the family settled down in Charleston, South Carolina.

Vanessa said that during her frequent travels, she had to change several schools. She participated in some extracurricular activities, including cheerleading, and she also volunteered to work for numerous local charities.

In 1986, an unfortunate incident happened with her family, and it was disturbing for the children. Her parents decided to get divorced and filed a case in court. 

The court’s outcome came in Vanessa’s mother, Helen’s favor. The court concluded to their father that the “children are allowed to meet their father on weekends.”

After the divorce, her mother remarried another man, and the family moved to Turkey. Her stepfather was enrolled in the U.S. Air Force.

Vanessa said that she had to evacuate the place as Saddam Hussein invaded Kuwait in 1990. Helen and the kids came back to the U.S. She left the kids to her father until she could arrange a decent job to support her kids. 

After the family had survived their tough time, Vanessa turned her attention to the Miss South Carolina Teen USA beauty pageant in 1996. 

From her childhood, she loved cheerleading, and she was eager to perform in front of an audience. 

Her skills and the natural persona she brightens in her performance lead her to win over the judges, sweeping all the categories before winning the crown.

Later, she practiced for the next year’s pageant. CBS performed live coverage of the show. The show began with a speech from President Bill Clinton.

At the show’s final moment, the audience was highly anticipating that Vanessa would win the competition. The suspense was kept by the host for some time until the audience got what they expected. 

Vanessa won her second pageant of Miss South Carolina Teen USA. “The two years were Vanessa’s best year“, Vanessa said. 

Vanessa Lachey Ethnicity

Vanessa is of English and Irish descent on her father’s side. She received some English and German descent from her mother’s side.

Apart from this, her mother was a Filipino, so she also had some ethnicities from her.

Vanessa Lachey was raised Catholic. She attended the School for Creative and Performing Arts in Cincinnati. 

Later, she attended Miami University in Oxford, Ohio, where she was a member of the Zeta Tau Alpha sorority and got her Bachelor of Fine Arts degree in Broadcast Journalism in 2000. 

Vanessa Lachey began her career as a television presenter for MTV’s Total Request Live from 2003-2006. 

She has also appeared as a participant on Dancing with the Stars and as a host for The Wendy Williams Show. 

Lachey married singer Nick Lachey in 2011, with whom she has three children. She is currently starring in the Nickelodeon series Safe Havens.
