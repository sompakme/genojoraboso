BearTek’s Entrepreneurial Journey on Shark Tank

Willie and Tarik, the entrepreneurs behind Beartek Bluetooth Smart Gloves, confidently entered the Shark Tank seeking $500,000 for 5% equity in their business. With their innovative gloves in hand, they showcased how the product revolutionized smartphone control without the need for direct touch. In the process, they revealed a remarkable tale of success, having already sold 1,500 units and generating an impressive $200,000 in profit within a span of just 15 months.

Tarik had invested $500,000 of his own funds to bring the business to life. The lucrative revenue projections and the gloves’ growing popularity had convinced the duo that their venture was poised for further success. However, the Sharks hesitated as they scrutinized the proposed $10 million valuation. They expressed concerns about the company’s reliance solely on gloves and urged expansion into other product lines.

“We love the product and the revenue it’s generating, but it’s a lot of money for something that’s limited to gloves.”

This apprehension ultimately led to no deal being made on the show. Despite the setback, BearTek’s appearance on Shark Tank provided valuable exposure and brought attention to their groundbreaking product.

Current Status of Beartek Bluetooth Smart Gloves

Unfortunately, Beartek Bluetooth Smart Gloves did not experience continued success after their appearance on Shark Tank. Despite their initial sales and the attention they received, the company faced challenges in sustaining their business.

Beartek launched an IndieGoGo campaign in an effort to generate additional revenue and support. The campaign successfully raised $60,000, but ultimately, the company made the decision to refund all of the backers’ funds.

Currently, Beartek’s website is no longer active, suggesting a decline in their business operations. However, the gloves are still available for purchase on Amazon, providing an opportunity for interested consumers to acquire this unique product.

Despite these challenges, it is unclear whether Beartek Bluetooth Smart Gloves is still in business. The future of the company remains uncertain, leaving both customers and investors in a state of anticipation.
