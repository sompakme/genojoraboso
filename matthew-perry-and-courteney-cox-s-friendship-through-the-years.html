Matthew Perry and Courteney Coxs Friendship Through the Years

Publish date: 2024-09-06

Matthew Perry and Courteney Cox’s chemistry as Chandler Bing and Monica Geller on Friends was so strong that many fans wondered if the actors had an offscreen romantic connection.

Despite falling in love and getting married on the NBC sitcom, Perry and Cox’s real-world relationship was always platonic.

In fact, Perry and Cox gave fans several glimpses at their friendship by reuniting on camera multiple times after Friends ended in 2004.

Cox gushed over Perry in January 2014 after he made a cameo on her TBS show Cougar Town. “It was so great to work with him. He’s so sweet,” she said of her longtime friend during an appearance on CONAN. “When I worked with him on Friends, he literally gave me my personality for 10 years.”
