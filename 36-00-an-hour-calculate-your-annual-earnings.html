    If you often work overtime, it’s important to consider that your annual earnings may increase due to extra pay. When you earn $36.00 an hour and work 40 hours per week, your annual salary would be approximately $74,880. However, if you work additional hours beyond the standard 40 hours, you may be eligible for overtime pay.

    Overtime pay is typically calculated at a higher rate than your regular hourly wage. The exact rate may vary depending on your employment contract or state laws. For example, you might earn 1.5 times your regular rate for each hour worked beyond 40 hours in a week.
