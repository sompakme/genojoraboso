In 1985, Armstrong formed his first band, Basic Radio, showcasing his passion for punk rock. Two years later, in 1987, he co-founded the influential band Operation Ivy, which gained a dedicated following through their energetic performances and politically charged lyrics.

However, after the disbandment of Operation Ivy, Armstrong faced personal challenges and struggles. It was during this difficult time that he found solace in music and formed Rancid with bassist Matt Freeman.
