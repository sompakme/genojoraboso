Irineo Alvarez Net Worth: Irineo Alvarez is a Mexican actor who has a net worth of $5 million. Irineo Alvarez was born in Ures, Sonora, Mexico in November 1967. He has acting in both films and soap operas. Irineo studied in Mexico City and ended up studying acting. He made his debut in the TV series Ladron de Corazones in 2003.

In 2004 he starred as Citius in the short Juegos polimpicos. In 2005 he made his feature film debut starring as Manuel in the movie The Three Burials of Melquiades Estrada. He starred in the film Bordertown as Domingo Esparza in 2006 and as Senor Doblado in the movie Under the Same Moon in 2007. In 2012 Alvarez starred as General Vega in the television series Capadocia. He has also starred in the films El hijo de Judas, Flor de fango, Ladies Nice, and The Last Call. Alvarez has starred in the TV series Cambio de vida, Gregoria la cucaracha, Mujer comprada, El Encanto del Aguila, Rosa Diamante, La Ruta Blanca, La Patrona, Fortuna, Las Trampas del Deseo, and El Senor de los Cielos.
