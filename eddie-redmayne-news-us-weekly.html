Eddie Redmayne News - Us Weekly

Publish date: 2024-09-15

Name:Eddie Redmayne

Birthday:January 6, 1982

About

Best known for his career-defining roles in 2012’s Les Miserables and 2014’s The Theory of Everything, this London-born actor originally got his start on the stage in English productions of shows like Twelfth Night and Red.

Besides his acting, Redmayne has also modeled for Burberry with models Alex Pettyfer and Cara Delevingne. 

In 2015, Redmayne won a Golden Globe and an Oscar for Best Actor for his role as Stephen Hawking in The Theory of Everything. In December 2014, the actor married publicist Hannah Bagshawe.
