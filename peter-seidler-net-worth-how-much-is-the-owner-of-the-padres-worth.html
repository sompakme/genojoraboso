Peter Seidler Net Worth: How much is the owner of the Padres worth?

Publish date: 2024-08-18

Peter Seidler is a businessman from the United States. He is the Major League Baseball chairman for the San Diego Padres.

Seidler is the grandson of former Los Angeles Dodgers owner Walter O’Malley and the nephew of Peter O’Malley.

He graduated from the University of Virginia with a bachelor’s degree in finance and a master’s degree in business administration from the University of California, Los Angeles.

Seidler is the managing partner of Seidler Equity Partners, a private equity firm based in Marina del Rey, California.

Seidler, his uncle Peter, and Ron Fowler formed the O’Malley Group in 2012 and bought the Padres from John Moores. Major League Baseball approved the transfer on November 18, 2020.
