American businessman Romesh T. Wadhwani has an estimated net worth of $2.8 billion as of April 2016 according to Forbes. He is best associated with Symphony Technology Group (STG) where he currently serves as the Chairman, founder and Chief executive officer. Symphony Technology Group is actually a strategic private equity firm that is geared towards building technology, software and internet-enabled services companies. In less than 10 years, the group is now composed of 12 companies that generate an accumulated revenue of around $2.5 billion.

Currently serving on the Board of Information Resources Inc., Romesh T. Wadhwani also sits as the executive chairman of MSC Software Inc., Shopzilla Inc., Symphony Health Solutions Inc., and Symphony Teleca Corporation.

However, prior to his success with Symphony Technology Group, Wadhwani has been a founder of two companies where he also served as the CEO and Chairman. One company specialized in offering technology-enabled energy management solutions while the other one worked on producing software and solutions for computer-integrated manufacturing.

Romesh T. Wandhwani also founded Aspect Development, Inc. in 1991 where he later served as the CEO and Chairman. Through the years, the firm developed into one of the leading software, analytic and content solutions for supply chain management and optimization.
