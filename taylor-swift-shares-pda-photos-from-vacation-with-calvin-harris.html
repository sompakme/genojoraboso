Taylor Swift Shares PDA Photos From Vacation With Calvin Harris

Publish date: 2024-07-23

https://www.instagram.com/p/BC_jaOUDvGT/

Out of the woods — and into paradise! Taylor Swift shared a series of Instagram photos from her romantic tropical vacation with boyfriend Calvin Harris on Tuesday, March 15.

“That time when we finally took a vacation. @calvinharris,” the “Out of the Woods” singer, 26, captioned a video of herself jumping on an inflatable trampoline in the middle of the clear ocean water. In the clip, a bikini-clad Swift can be heard asking Harris, “Are you watching?” before she jumps off the trampoline and flops into the water. 

https://www.instagram.com/p/BC_jSSmjvGF/

“It hurt a lot,” Swift yells at Harris, who can be heard laughing while exclaiming, “S‑‑t!”

Swift captioned a rare PDA snap of the two kissing on the beach with a simple red-heart emoji, and also posted a snap of their initials scribbled in the white sand. “Taylor Swift + Adam Wiles,” her caption read alongside the photo, revealing the DJ’s real name.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BC_jm_uDvGk/

In another photo, Swift showed off her bikini bod, posing like a Victoria’s Secret model in the gorgeous water before a beach lined with palm trees.

https://www.instagram.com/p/BC_jtBajvGy/

Harris also shared snippets of their enviable vacation with fans. The producer posted a series of Snapchat videos of them on a swan floatie, admiring the gorgeous sunset. 

The couple recently celebrated their first anniversary. “3.6.15 One year down!” Swift captioned a photo of an engraved locket from her boyfriend on March 6.

Us Weekly exclusively confirmed the pair were dating in April 2015, after the duo hit it off at the 2015 Brit Awards. Days after the awards show, the couple were spotted in Nashville together and Swift flew to Las Vegas to watch Harris perform.
