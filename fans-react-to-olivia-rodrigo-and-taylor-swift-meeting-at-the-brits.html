Fans React To Olivia Rodrigo And Taylor Swift Meeting At The Brits!

Publish date: 2024-09-07

Olivia Rodrigo finally met her idol, Taylor Swift, at the Brit Awards! The DriversLicense singer is a longtime Swiftie and the singers have communicated over social media, with Swift referring to her as "baby" and Olivia rocking out to her music. Taylor has previously sent Olivia gifts and is a big fan of her music and skills as a young songwriter.

They Finally Met!

After months of interacting over Instagram, the High School Musical series actor finally met Taylor in-person, and shared photographic evidence of her meeting Swift! It looks like they had the best time ever!

Olivia shared a black and white image of Swift and herself, making silly faces at the Brits. She captioned the photo with puppy dog eye emojis and wrote "brits were a dream. never wanna leave!!!" to her Instagram story, with some glimpses from the coveted awards show.

Olivia's fans were thrilled to discover she had finally met Taylor, and were quick to express their happiness for her.

Musician Conan Gray, who is a close friend of Olivia's and a huge fan of Taylor's wrote "i love my sister and my mom." Previously, Taylor sent her "two kids" (Olivia and Conan) a sneak peek of her new version of You Belong With Me, ahead of its release.
