Who is Maurizio Gucci Girlfriend Paola Franchi?

Franchi and Gucci began dating in 1990 after meeting at a private club in Saint Moritz. For five years, Franchi became Gucci’s live-in companion, occupying a beautiful apartment in Milan’s Corso Venezia with the designer.

When Gucci and Reggiani divorced in 1994, they began planning a wedding at Gucci’s Saint Moritz house in Switzerland.
