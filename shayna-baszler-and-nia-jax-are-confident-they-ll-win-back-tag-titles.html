Shayna Baszler And Nia Jax Are Confident Theyll Win Back Tag Titles

Publish date: 2024-09-14

Shayna Baszler and Nia Jax aren’t too happy that they lost the WWE Women’s Tag Team Championships to Natalya and Tamina Snuka. On Monday Night RAW, they’ll get the chance to win them back, and are more ready.

During an interview backstage at last night’s episode of Friday Night SmackDown, the duo was asked how they felt about their upcoming title match. After forcing Natalya to tap out last night, Shayna Baszler said that the duo was underappreciated and “cursed” as a team, but that they will become the WWE Women’s Tag Team Champions for a third time on Monday.
