About Doug DeMuro

Douglas DeMuro, born on May 22, 1988, currently resides in San Diego, California. He concentrates on the auto business; as of 2022, his YouTube channel for auto reviews had more than four million members.

DeMuro owns the auto-selling website Cars & Bids, which enables people to buy and sell cars in online auctions and his YouTube ventures. 

After leaving his previous work as a writer and editor of the auto blog Oversteer on Autotrader.com, he started the company in 2020.

DeMuro previously contributed to Jalopnik and The Truth About Cars. While Doug was a Resident Advisor at Dobbs Hall, they became friends.

He paid tetrachromat artist and color expert Concetta Antico $1.7 million for a home in the Mission Hills neighborhood in 2019.

Around 2016, Doug started producing YouTube content while working as an AutoTraders supervisor. His channel featured reviews of several automakers, like Portage GT, Tesla, Buggati Chiron, and others.
