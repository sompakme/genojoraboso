How Did Brad Williams Meet His Wife, Jasmine Williams?

The story of how Brad Williams met his wife, Jasmine, is unique and intriguing, diverging from the typical narrative of romantic encounters. Much like celebrities who embrace dating apps, Brad and Jasmine met through a dating app, Feeld, which is known for facilitating connections between polyamorous and non-monogamous people.

Brad was originally using the app with the intention of organizing a threesome with another woman he had connected with. However, when the time for the meeting arrived, the other woman canceled, leaving Brad and Jasmine to meet alone. This unexpected turn of events led to a surprising and immediate connection between the two.

During a podcast session with 98 Rock Baltimore, Brad recalled their first meeting with enthusiasm, describing it as an incredible experience where "sparks flew." The first date, despite or perhaps because of its unconventional beginnings, was a success. This encounter marked the beginning of their journey together.

Interestingly, the couple continued to engage in polyamorous relationships during the early years of their dating. Brad and Jasmine eventually decided to commit to a monogamous relationship and got married on September 6, 2017. This marked a new chapter in their lives, transitioning from the open nature of their initial relationship to a more traditional marriage.

Aside from their unique love story, Jasmine and Brad's relationship is also notable for their height difference. Jasmine stands at 5 feet 6 inches, towering over Brad, who has dwarfism. However, this has never hindered their deep bond.
