Here's What Former 'Bachelor' Ben Higgins' Life Became After 'The Bachelor'

Publish date: 2024-08-14

From being a contestant in the eleventh season of The Bachelorette, to being announced as the bachelor of the twentieth season of The Bachelor during Bachelor In Paradise: After Paradise, Ben Higgins has certainly left his mark in Bachelor Nation. During his season, fans watched as he developed strong feelings towards both Lauren Bushnell and JoJo Fletcher. In the season finale, Ben came under fire for telling both women that he loved them, but he ended up leaving the show happily engaged to Lauren Bushnell.

Lauren and Ben settled together in Denver and starred in their own spin-off Ben and Lauren: Happily Ever After? The two, however, broke-up in 2017. Lauren later expressed that she was not keen on the spin-off, owing to the fact that she does not like all eyes to be on her. Ben Higgins and Lauren Bushnell ended things peacefully and have both since moved on.

8 Ben Higgins Moved Back To Indiana

Ben Higgins revealed that he left Colorado, where he has a home, and moved back to Indiana due to a mental breakdown. During his appearance on former Bachelorette, Trista Sutter's Better Etc. podcast, Ben explained that he was grappling with his fame and where his image as a former Bachelor fits in his life going forward.

He admitted to being "infatuated" with fame. "I started to really feel like, ‘Oh, my gosh, this is the thing that I was needing to fill me. This is the thing that will change the rest of my life. I'll be in the in-group forever now,' he said.

7 Ben Higgins Got Married

Ben Higgins married Jessica Clarke in November 2021, after just under three years of being in a relationship. The couple met in 2018, after Ben slid into Jessica's DM on Instagram. He officially introduced Jessica to his followers in February 2019.

The two were initially in a long-distance relationship but later on went ahead to buy a house together in Colorado after becoming engaged. This newly married couple looks promising, and fans are excited to see what the future holds for them.

6 Ben Higgins Published His Book

Ben Higgins became a published author in 2021, after he published his book, "Alone in Plain Sight: Searching for Connection When You're Seen But Not Known." The book talks about Ben's struggles behind the facade of "looking like he has it all together," and his feelings of dissatisfaction, fear, and deep loneliness.

In the book, Ben also explores ways to understand ourselves more deeply so that we are free to connect with others, how shared pain can bridge even the widest gaps between two very different people, why we must deconstruct our culture’s fairy-tale view of love, and how the God who longs for relationship with us is the answer to our need for connection.

5 Ben Higgins' Podcast

'Hope Still Wins' is a podcast that aims at unifying the world through the lens of hope, one thought-leader and story at a time. Ben discusses topics ranging from faith and music to mental health and racial injustice. Each guest shares personal stories that have shaped their current view on hope, ultimately answering the question, “Does hope still win? Why or why not?”

4 Ben Higgins Co-owns Restaurants

From a childhood dream to reality, Ben Higgins co-owns Ash’Kara, a restaurant known for showcasing Middle Eastern cuisine. This dream was inspired by memories from a restaurant in Leesburg, Indiana. Although Ben's restaurants were struggling during the wake of COVID, he is hopeful that things are looking up now.

3 Ben Co-Founded A Coffee Company

Ben Higgins launched Generous Coffee Company to help fund operational expenses of non-profits whose mission is to help the world. Ben calls the business "for purpose" instead of "for profit." The company has three founders, as well as a director of communications and over fifty volunteers. It has been operational since 2018, and is run out of Denver, Colorado.

2 'Slice Of Home' With Ben Higgins

Ben Higgins was part of a social media campaign called "Slice of Home" celebrating homemade meals, family, and community, in collaboration with Rudi's Rocky Mountain Bakery.

For Ben, food has been an important way for people to connect and celebrate each other's company. The campaign's focus was on making memories breaking bread together. Ben said he will share his family's sandwich recipes, along with how he enjoys them with friends, and also added that the campaign will include several giveaways.

1 Ben Higgins' Political Career

Shortly after his season of The Bachelor, Ben Higgins decided to run for a seat in the Colorado House of Representatives. This, however, never came to be as Ben released a statement saying, "I regret that I must withdraw my candidacy. Despite my best efforts to pursue this opportunity in good faith, I recently received information that has made such a pursuit unworkable. Due to unforeseen circumstances, I will not be able to move forward as a candidate. I find solace in knowing that our intentions and actions have been fair and sincere. I entered into this endeavor wanting to bring positive change to my community, and it is with that same spirit that I will move forward, albeit on a different path."
