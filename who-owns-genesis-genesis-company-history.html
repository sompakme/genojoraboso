Who Owns Genesis? Genesis Company History

Publish date: 2024-09-22

Genesis Motor, LLC, commonly referred to as Genesis, is the luxury vehicle brand of the South Korean vehicle manufacturer Hyundai Motor Company. It was officially announced as an independent marque in 2015. The company is headquartered in Seoul, South Korea, and is led by Won-Hee Lee as the President and CEO. Genesis vehicles are designed in Germany, South Korea, and the United States, and produced in South Korea.

Key Takeaways:

  • Genesis is the luxury vehicle brand of Hyundai Motor Company.
  • The brand was established as an independent marque in 2015.
  • Genesis headquarters is located in Seoul, South Korea.
  • Genesis vehicles are designed in Germany, South Korea, and the United States.
  • The vehicles are produced in South Korea.

Genesis Conception and Under Hyundai

In 2003, Hyundai conceived the “Concept Genesis” as the precursor to the luxury brand we know today as Genesis. This groundbreaking initiative aimed to create a line of premium vehicles that would compete with established luxury car manufacturers.

The first model in the Genesis lineup, the Genesis sedan, was introduced in 2007. Its sleek design, advanced technology, and exceptional performance garnered attention and praise from both critics and consumers alike.

YearEvent

As the Genesis project progressed, there was an internal debate within Hyundai about how to position this new line of luxury vehicles. The company had to decide whether to sell the Genesis as a Hyundai model or to launch it as a separate brand altogether.

After much consideration, Hyundai made the decision to introduce the Genesis as a Hyundai model in 2008. This move allowed Hyundai to leverage its established reputation and dealer network, while still offering a premium experience to its customers.

Today, the Genesis brand has evolved into a renowned luxury car manufacturer, known for its exceptional craftsmanship, cutting-edge technology, and unparalleled driving experience.

Genesis as a Stand-alone Brand

In 2015, Genesis made a significant transition and became a stand-alone brand. This decision was driven by the company’s remarkable success in the luxury car market over the past seven years, its exceptional performance compared to other segment sellers, and the growing demand from customers for an independent division.

As a testament to its commitment to luxury and innovation, Genesis unveiled its first model, the Genesis G90, in December 2015. This flagship vehicle showcased the brand’s dedication to exquisite craftsmanship, advanced technology, and unparalleled comfort.

To ensure a seamless introduction into the market, Genesis adopted a unique distribution strategy. The brand’s vehicles are sold through a select number of existing Hyundai dealerships in the United States, with dedicated space and trained personnel to cater specifically to the needs of Genesis customers.

This strategic move allowed Genesis to leverage the existing infrastructure and customer base of Hyundai while establishing its brand identity as a separate and prestigious entity. By doing so, Genesis could effectively capture the attention and loyalty of luxury car enthusiasts, offering them a distinct and exclusive experience with every Genesis model.

Genesis Models

Genesis offers a range of luxury sedan and SUV models. The brand’s lineup showcases their commitment to innovation, design, and performance.

Flagship Model: Genesis G90

The Genesis G90 is the flagship sedan model, representing the pinnacle of luxury and elegance. With its refined aesthetics, advanced technology, and exceptional comfort, the G90 sets the standard for the Genesis brand.

Genesis G80

The Genesis G80 embodies a perfect balance of performance and luxury. Its dynamic design, premium amenities, and cutting-edge features make it a compelling choice for those seeking a sophisticated driving experience.

Genesis G70

The Genesis G70 is a compact luxury sedan that delivers thrilling performance and agile handling. Designed with meticulous attention to detail, the G70 combines sportiness with elegance, appealing to drivers who crave both excitement and refinement.

Genesis GV80

The Genesis GV80 is the brand’s first SUV, offering versatility and space without compromising on style or performance. With its bold design, advanced safety features, and luxurious interior, the GV80 redefines what an SUV can be.

Electrified G80

In line with the brand’s commitment to sustainability, Genesis unveiled its first production electric vehicle, the Electrified G80, in 2021. This model combines the exceptional performance of the G80 with the efficiency and environmental benefits of electric power.

Genesis models are named using an alphanumeric convention, with sedans starting with the letter “G” and crossovers starting with “GV.” This naming scheme reflects the brand’s commitment to clarity and consistency in its vehicle lineup.

Genesis Personnel

Genesis Motor, LLC, boasts a team of talented individuals who contribute to the brand’s success. Led by the global head and executive vice president, Jaehoon (Jay) Chang, the Genesis team includes industry experts with diverse backgrounds and skills.

One notable figure is Peter Schreyer, the chief creative officer, who has played a pivotal role in shaping Genesis’ design language. With his extensive experience in the automotive industry, Schreyer brings a unique vision to the brand’s aesthetic.

Heading the operations in North America is Claudia Marquez, the chief operating officer of Genesis North America. Marquez spearheads the brand’s growth and development in the region, ensuring seamless operations and customer satisfaction.

Overseas, Markus Henne serves as the CEO of Genesis Motor China, leading the brand’s endeavors in one of the world’s largest automotive markets. Henne’s expertise and strategic approach contribute to Genesis’ expansion and success in China.

In terms of design, SangYup Lee serves as the head of design, responsible for bringing Genesis’ innovative concepts to life. Lee’s visionary approach and meticulous attention to detail ensure that every Genesis vehicle exudes sophistication and style.

Genesis Global Sales

Since its launch, Genesis has been making significant strides in global sales, solidifying its position in the luxury vehicle market. From November 2015 to October 2018, the brand successfully sold a total of 206,882 vehicles, demonstrating strong consumer demand for Genesis models.

Among the Genesis lineup, the G80 emerged as the most popular model, with an impressive sales count of 127,283 units. The G90 followed closely behind with 52,417 units sold, showcasing its appeal in the luxury sedan market. The G70, a more compact option, saw a commendable sales figure of 27,182 units.

In addition to these remarkable sales figures, Genesis achieved a significant milestone in 2016 by selling 66,029 vehicles in South Korea alone, further solidifying its presence in its home country.

Looking to the future, Genesis has set its sights on expanding its global presence, with a particular focus on the European market. The brand aims to captivate luxury car enthusiasts around the world and continue its upward trajectory in sales and customer satisfaction.

Genesis Marketing

Genesis has established a strong presence in the marketing arena, engaging with various events and organizations to promote its luxury brand. As an official sponsor, Genesis has partnered with esteemed platforms such as the NFL, the Genesis Invitational golf tournament, the Scottish Open, and the Condé Nast International Conference, among others. These collaborations allow Genesis to connect with a diverse audience and showcase its commitment to excellence and innovation.

Recognizing the importance of architectural design, Genesis has also joined forces with renowned architect Rem Koolhaas to create cutting-edge showrooms. These spaces reflect the brand’s ethos of sophistication and elegance, providing customers with an immersive and visually stunning experience. With a focus on creating unique atmospheres, the collaboration between Genesis and Rem Koolhaas highlights the brand’s dedication to exceptional craftsmanship.

Furthermore, Genesis offers cloud-based services to enhance customer convenience and vehicle functionality. Genesis Connected Services allow users to remotely control certain vehicle features, access vehicle diagnostics, and even schedule service appointments. The brand’s Intelligent Assistant further enhances the driving experience by providing personalized recommendations and assistance, ensuring that every journey is nothing short of extraordinary.
