DLo Brown Teases Return Of Aces &amp; Eights To IMPACT Wrestling

Publish date: 2024-07-26

The infamous Total Nonstop Action faction Aces & Eights could be returning to IMPACT Wrestling.

After the credits of tonight’s episode, D’Lo Brown is shown sitting on a chair and talking to someone. “Yeah, I understand,” says Brown. “Perception is reality. But I don’t like being called a corporate stooge in any way. I’d like the band to get back together. We’ll talk tomorrow.” Brown then gets up and the Aces & Eights vest is on the chair, he then comes back and grabs it.

If you’re not aware, the Aces & Eights faction debuted in June 2012 and lasted until November 2013. Members included Bully Ray, who served as the president, Brooke Tessmacher, Garett Bischoff, Knux, Taz, Devon, D’Lo Brown, D.O.C., Ivelisse, Mr. Anderson, Tito Ortiz, and Wes Brisco.
